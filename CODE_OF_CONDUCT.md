# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

- Be welcoming, kind, and patient.
- Assume good intent. Disagreements are normal; personal attacks are not.
- Harassment of any kind is not tolerated.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers via a private channel: open a GitHub Discussion marked `private` or contact the repository owner directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
